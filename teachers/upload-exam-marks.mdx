# Upload Exam Marks

This page explains how teachers can upload exam marks for their students.

## How to Upload Exam Marks

1. Go to **Teachers > Upload Exam Marks** in the sidebar.
2. Select the exam and class, then enter marks for each student.
3. Save your entries.
