# Assignment Submission

This page explains how teachers can view and manage assignment submissions from students.

## How to Manage Assignment Submissions

1. Go to **Teachers > Assignment Submission** in the sidebar.
2. View the list of submitted assignments and their status.
3. Grade and provide feedback as needed.
