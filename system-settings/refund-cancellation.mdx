# Refund & Cancellation

This page explains how to configure refund and cancellation policies for your School CRM system.

## How to Update Refund & Cancellation Policies

1. Go to **System Settings > Refund & Cancellation** in the sidebar.
2. Edit the policy text and settings as needed.
3. Save your changes.
