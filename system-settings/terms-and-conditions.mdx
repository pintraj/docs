# Terms and Conditions

This page explains how to update the terms and conditions for your School CRM system.

## How to Update Terms and Conditions

1. Go to **System Settings > Terms and Conditions** in the sidebar.
2. Edit the terms and conditions text as needed.
3. Save your changes.
