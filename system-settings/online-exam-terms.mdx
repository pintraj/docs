# Online Exam Terms and Conditions

This page explains how to set the terms and conditions for online exams.

## How to Update Online Exam Terms and Conditions

1. Go to **System Settings > Online Exam Terms and Conditions** in the sidebar.
2. Edit the terms and conditions text as needed.
3. Save your changes.
