# Expert Teachers

This page explains how to update the Expert Teachers section, which highlights your teaching staff.

## How to Update Expert Teachers

1. Go to **Web Settings > Expert Teachers** in the sidebar.
2. Add or edit teacher profiles, including names, photos, and descriptions.
3. Save your changes.
