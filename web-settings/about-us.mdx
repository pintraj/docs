# About Us

This page explains how to update the About Us section, including the title, heading, description, image, and status.

## How to Update About Us

1. Go to **Web Settings > About Us** in the sidebar.
2. Fill in the following fields:
   - **Title**
   - **Heading**
   - **Description**
   - **Image** (recommended size: 645px Ã— 555px)
   - **Status** (Enable/Disable)
3. Click **Upload** to add an image.
4. Save your changes.
