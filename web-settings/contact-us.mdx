# Contact Us

This page explains how to update the Contact Us section, where users can find your school's contact information or submit inquiries.

## How to Update Contact Us

1. Go to **Web Settings > Contact Us** in the sidebar.
2. Edit the contact details and form fields as needed.
3. Save your changes.
