# Education Program

This page explains how to update the Education Program section, including the title, heading, description, and status.

## How to Update Education Program

1. Go to **Web Settings > Education Program** in the sidebar.
2. Fill in the following fields:
   - **Title**
   - **Heading**
   - **Description**
   - **Status** (Enable/Disable)
3. Save your changes.
