# Manage Online Exam

This page explains how teachers can manage online exams for their classes.

## How to Manage Online Exams

1. Go to **Teachers > Manage Online Exam** in the sidebar.
2. Create, edit, or delete online exams as needed.
3. Monitor exam status and student participation.
