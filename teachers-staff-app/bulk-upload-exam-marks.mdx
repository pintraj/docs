# Bulk Upload Exam Marks

This page explains how teachers can bulk upload exam marks for their students.

## How to Bulk Upload Exam Marks

1. Go to **Teachers > Bulk Upload Exam Marks** in the sidebar.
2. Download the template, fill in the marks, and upload the file.
3. Save your changes.
