# Creating Your School CRM Account

Welcome to School CRM! This guide will walk you through the process of creating your account and setting up your school's profile. The entire process takes just a few minutes.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A valid email address
- Your school's basic information (name, address, contact details)
- Administrator access to set up the initial account

## Step 1: Visit School CRM Website

1. Open your web browser and navigate to [https://schoolcrm.live](https://schoolcrm.live)
2. Click the **"Get Started"** or **"Sign Up"** button in the top navigation

<Callout type="info">
  You can also access the signup page directly at [https://schoolcrm.live/signup](https://schoolcrm.live/signup)
</Callout>

## Step 3: Fill Out Registration Form

Complete the registration form with your information:

### Personal Information
- **Full Name**: Enter your full name as it will appear in the system
- **Email Address**: Use a professional email address (this will be your login)
- **Phone Number**: Provide a contact number for account verification

### School Information
- **School Name**: Enter your institution's official name
- **School Type**: Select from options like:
  - Primary School
  - Secondary School
  - High School
  - College/University
  - Special Education
  - Other
- **Country**: Select your country from the dropdown
- **State/Province**: Enter your state or province
- **City**: Enter your city
- **Address**: Provide your school's complete address

### Account Security
- **Password**: Create a strong password (minimum 8 characters)
- **Confirm Password**: Re-enter your password
- **Terms of Service**: Check the box to agree to terms
- **Privacy Policy**: Check the box to agree to privacy policy

<Callout type="warning">
  **Important**: Use a strong password that includes uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters for better security.
</Callout>

## Step 4: Email Verification

1. Check your email inbox for a verification email from School CRM
2. Click the **"Verify Email"** link in the email
3. You'll be redirected back to School CRM with your account activated

<Callout type="info">
  If you don't see the verification email, check your spam folder. You can also request a new verification email from the login page.
</Callout>

## Step 5: Complete School Profile Setup

After email verification, you'll be guided through a setup wizard:

### Basic School Information
- **School Logo**: Upload your school's logo (optional but recommended)
- **School Colors**: Choose colors that match your school's branding
- **Academic Year**: Set the current academic year
- **Time Zone**: Select your school's time zone

### Initial Configuration
- **Default Currency**: Choose your preferred currency for financial transactions
- **Date Format**: Select your preferred date format
- **Language**: Choose your preferred language (multiple languages supported)

## Step 6: Create Your First Admin User

Set up your administrator account:

- **Username**: Choose a username for your admin account
- **Role**: Select "Administrator" as your role
- **Permissions**: Review and confirm administrator permissions

## Account Security Best Practices

Once your account is created, follow these security best practices:

- **Use Strong Passwords**: Create unique, strong passwords for all accounts
- **Enable Two-Factor Authentication**: Add an extra layer of security to your account
- **Regular Password Updates**: Change your password every 90 days
- **Monitor Account Activity**: Regularly review login activity and account settings
- **Train Staff**: Ensure all staff members understand security protocols
