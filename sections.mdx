## Class Sections Management

School CRM allows you to create and manage multiple sections (A, B, C, D, etc.) for each class. This helps in organizing students into smaller groups for better classroom management.

![Section Management](/images/section.png)

### Adding Sections

You can easily add new sections through the section management interface. Common section names include:
- Section A
- Section B 
- Section C
- Section D
