# Create Expense

The **Create Expense** feature allows you to record and track individual expenses for your school. You can assign each expense to a category, enter details, and keep your financial records organized.

---

## How to Create an Expense

1. **Go to:**  
   **Expense** â†’ **Create Expense**
2. **Enter Expense Details:**
   - **Select Category:** Choose the relevant expense category (required)
   - **Title:** Enter a title for the expense (required)
   - **Reference No.:** (Optional) Enter a reference number for tracking
   - **Amount:** Enter the expense amount (required)
   - **Date:** Select the date of the expense (required)
   - **Description:** Add a description for the expense (optional)
   - **Select Session Year:** Choose the academic session (required)
3. **Submit:**
   - Click **Submit** to save the expense
   - Use **Reset** to clear the form

---

## Best Practices
- Assign each expense to the correct category for accurate reporting
- Use clear titles and descriptions
- Record expenses promptly to keep records up to date

---

## Troubleshooting
- **Cannot Add Expense?**  
  Ensure all required fields are filled
- **Expense Not Showing?**  
  Refresh the page or check filters
